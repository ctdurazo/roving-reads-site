About RovingReads

Built for real book clubs (not “one-off” groups)

The problem

Book clubs usually live across group chats, notes apps, spreadsheets, and a dozen links. It’s hard to remember what you read, what you rated, what’s next, and what everyone wants to read.

Our approach

RovingReads brings discovery, scheduling, and club ratings together. Keep it lightweight, fast, and friendly— with just enough structure to keep the club moving.

Features

Simple now, extensible later
